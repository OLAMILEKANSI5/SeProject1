About Cross River

Cross Rivers State is in Southern Nigeria, just along the coastal region and is bordered to the east by Cameroon. It has an estimated population of about 4 million people and the main tribes are Efik, Bekwarra and Ejagham. Its Capital city, Calabar, is among the finest cities in Nigeria and is home to the famous Calabar Carnival.
